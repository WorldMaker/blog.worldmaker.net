I saw a sneak showing of A Scanner Darkly last night. I thought it was great. It really touched at a lot of the heart of the Phillip K. Dick novel and had some really good acting performances. I was impressed by the animation and thought it did a lot to help keep the feel of the movie close to that of the book. I'm definitely adding this movie to my DVD Collection.
